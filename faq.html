Legal Policies
Last updated — June 2026

Acceptance of Terms

By engaging Ritik & Co. for any service, you agree to these Terms and Conditions. These terms govern all projects, proposals, and ongoing relationships between Ritik & Co. and its clients.

Services

Ritik & Co. provides creative production and digital marketing services including but not limited to 3D visualization, video production, paid advertising, social media management, and website design. The specific scope of each project is defined in the individual proposal or agreement.

Payment

All projects require a minimum 50% advance payment before work begins. The remaining balance is due upon delivery of final files. Ongoing retainer services are billed monthly in advance.

Revisions

Each project includes a defined number of revision rounds as stated in the proposal. Additional revisions beyond the agreed scope will be billed at our standard hourly rate.

Intellectual Property

Upon full payment, all intellectual property rights for the delivered work transfer to the client. Ritik & Co. reserves the right to display the work in our portfolio unless a confidentiality agreement is in place.

Confidentiality

We treat all client information as strictly confidential and do not share business details, strategies, or data with third parties without explicit written consent.

Limitation of Liability

Ritik & Co. is not liable for any indirect, incidental, or consequential damages arising from the use of our services. Our total liability is limited to the amount paid for the specific project in question.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly — including your name, email address, company name, and project details when you fill out a form or contact us. We do not collect payment information directly; all payments are processed through secure third-party platforms.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your enquiries and deliver project proposals
  • To communicate project updates and deliverables
  • To send relevant service information (you can opt out at any time)
  • To improve our website and services

Data Storage

Your information is stored securely and is only accessible to the Ritik & Co. team. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Cookies

Contact

Project Cancellation Before Work Begins

If a project is cancelled before any creative work has started, you are entitled to a full refund of any advance payment made, minus any administrative or processing fees.

Project Cancellation After Work Has Begun

Once creative work has commenced, refunds are evaluated based on the percentage of work completed. The client will be charged for work completed up to the point of cancellation, and any remaining balance will be refunded.

Revision & Dissatisfaction

We are committed to delivering work that meets the agreed brief. If you are not satisfied with a deliverable, we will first work to resolve the issue through revisions within the agreed scope. Refunds based on dissatisfaction are considered only if we are unable to meet the requirements outlined in the original brief after reasonable revision attempts.

Retainer Services

Monthly retainer payments are non-refundable once the service period has begun. Retainers can be cancelled with 15 days written notice before the next billing cycle.

Digital Products & Strategy Documents

Strategy documents, proposals, and digital deliverables that have been delivered and accessed are non-refundable.

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, contact us at contact@ritikandco.com with your project details and reason for the request. We will respond within 3 business days.
